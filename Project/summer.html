Kits Beach

Not only arguably the best beach in Vancouver, Kits Beach also has grassy areas for picnics and plenty of tasty food options within walking distance.

Wreck Beach

Located at UBC, Wrench Beach is a well known, clothing-optional, beach.

Biking

Vancouver is a very bike friendly city. Rent a bike and explore the city! Don't forget to cycle around Stanley Park.

Hiking

Outside of the downtown core, there are dozens of trails to explore. Personal recommendations include Golden Ears, Quarry Rock, and the Grouse Grind.

Squamish

Squamish, which is an hour drive from Vancouver, is an amazing town with activities including white water rafting, fishing, and hiking.

The hike to Garibaldi lake is a must. Takes an entire day, but the view at the top is beautiful.
