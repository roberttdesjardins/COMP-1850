Attractions

Granville Island

Granville Island is a beautiful peninsula with a world renown public market and great beer from local breweries.

Rating: 4.8/5

Richmond Night Market

The Richmond Night Market is a great place to get food, trinkets, and entertainment. Try unique snacks like rotatos, and deep-fried ice-cream.

Only open during summer weekends.

Rating: 3.7/5

PNE

The PNE is a thrilling amusement park with world class rides and entertainment. Don't miss out on the mini donuts!

Rides are open during the sumemr months, and there are special halloween themed attractions during October.

Rating: 4.4/5

UBC

The University of British Columbia is isolated from the rest of Vancouver and is a great location for its beautiful scenery and gardens. Be sure to visit the Museum of Anthropology, shown to the left, for an educational experience of First Nations culture.

Rating: 4.2/5

Britannia Mine

The Britannia Mine Museum was once a functional mine which has since been turned into a museum focused on the mining history of Vancouver. Take a mine cart into the mine, and see the mining tools demonstraited which were used throughout the mines history. Afterwards, there are mining related activities such as panning for gold and the option to site in a massive mining dump truck.

Rating: 4.7/5

Gastown

Gastown is an area of Downtown Vancouver which has its own unique culture and atmosphere. A great place for food and drinks!

Rating: 4.4/5
