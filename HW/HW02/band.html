Ronnie James Dio Wikipedia Page

Overview:

DIO was a heavy metal band formed in 1982 when Ronnie James Dio and Vinny Appice left Black Sabbath due to an argument. The band released 10 studio albums throughout its history, its first and most famous album being Holy Diver. Ronnie died in 2010 due to cancer which resulted in the end of the band.

Members:

There are far too many band members to list, so I will pick and choose some of the more prominent members

  • Ronnie James Dio: Lead vocalist; the only constant member throughout the bands history
  • Vinny Appice: Drums; left Black Sabbath to start DIO with Ronnie
  • Jimmy Bain: Bass Guitar, Keyboard; co-wrote songs in many of the albums including the songs Holy Diver and Rainbow in the Dark
  • Simon Wright: Drums; Joined DIO after leaving AC/DC in 1998. Worked on some of the bands later albums

My personal favorite albums:

Holy Diver (1983)
The bands debut and most famous album, Holy Diver set a high benchmark for metal and rock bands and proved that Ronnie could stand on his own outside of Black Sabbath. It's title song, Holy Diver, and Don't Talk to Strangers are the two standouts from the album in my opinion.
Dream Evil (1987)
Dream Evil is the bands 4th album and marks a slight departure from the previous 3 albums by incorperating more melodic elements yet still retains the passion and metal elements which the band had become known for. Some of the standout songs form the album include Sunset Superman and Naked in the Rain.
Magica (2000)
Magica is the bands 8th album and is often regarded as a comeback album after a few less well recieved albums. Simon Wright and Jimmy Bain returned to DIO for this album. Magica tells a story of good vs. evil, which is a departure from previous albums which are more about standalone songs.
