Seymour Mountain

Seymour is the most economical choice for skiing or snowboarding, and is perfect for those looking to learn.

Grouse Mountain

Grouse Mountain has a lot to offer during the winter from skiing and snowboarding to snowshoeing and ice skating.

The view over the city is unmatched.

Cyrpus Mountain

Cyrpus is a great mountain for winter activities close to Vancouver.

Whistler and Blackcomb

For those who are more advanced or simply want more run variety, Whistler is an excellent mountain with its own culture and activities.

Accessable by a couple hour drive along the sea-to-sky highway.
