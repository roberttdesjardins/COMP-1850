Must See Sites!

The Capilano Suspension Bridge

The Capilano Suspension Bridge in North Vancouver is one of the most iconic attractions in the city. Over 130m long and 70m high, the bridge is a thrilling experience for all ages.

Rating: 4.4/5

The Vancouver Aquarium

As Canada's largest aquarium, the Vancouver Aquarium is a must see. Animals include dolphins, penguins, otters, and more! A educational and family fun experience.

Rating: 4.7/5

Stanley Park

Stanley Park is consistently rated as one of the world's best urban parks. Cycle around the park, have a picnic, or just enjoy the atmosphere!

Rating: 4.8/5

The Vancouver Art Gallery

The Vancouver Art Gallery is one of the largest art installations in Canada and displays many pieces by famous Canadians as well as world renown artists.

Rating: 4.2/5

Science World

Science World is one of Vancouvers most iconic buildings. An excellent destination for families, but it can be an educational experience for adults as well!

Rating: 4.7/5

Grouse Mountain

Grouse Mountain in North Vancouver is the closest destination for a day of skiing or snowboarding for anyone staying in Vancouver. In the summer the mountain is also a great for the famous Grouse Grind hike and the beautiful view over Vancouver.

Rating: 4.4/5
